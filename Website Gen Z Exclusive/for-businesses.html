How Nigh Drops Work

Make today a day they won't forget.

Most Drops go live the same day or the day before. That short window is exactly what makes customers act — before the moment is missed.

Step 01

Pick Your Moment

Set a time today, tomorrow, or any upcoming day. The shorter the broadcasting window, the more urgency customers feel to get a spot.

Step 02

Make It Worth It

Create a time-bound, in-person experience that can't be found anywhere else. Once the window closes or spots fill — it's gone.

Step 03

Spread the Word

Share a link, QR code, or text to get the word out. Customers have hours — not days — to get a spot. That's what turns interest into timely foot traffic.

Step 04

See Real Results

Real-time reservations and verified arrivals at the right time — not just views and clicks. You see exactly who showed up and when.
