Change is Nigh.

It's Time to Bring Back Local.

The Nigh Foundation advances innovations that strengthen local communities, real-life belonging, and shared prosperity in a rapidly changing world. We believe what matters most in life should feel close — close to home, close to community, close to opportunity.

Why We Exist

Strong local communities are the bedrock of a thriving society.

It happens across tables, in community centers, in classrooms, and at local stores, restaurants, studios, and venues. We build trust through shared space, conversation, laughter, eye contact, and collaboration. Technology can enhance that connection — but it cannot replace it.

Over time, opportunity, economic power, and decision-making have drifted away from local communities. Many families feel stretched. Many young people feel uncertain about their future. Many local businesses struggle to compete in systems optimized for scale rather than shared prosperity.

When local businesses succeed, communities gain stability, dignity, and hope. When more value circulates locally, society becomes stronger from the ground up.

Rebuilding strong local economies is not a rejection of innovation. It is the next step in human advancement — aligning technology and investment with broader prosperity and collective well-being.

Our Mission

Advance innovations that strengthen local communities.

The Foundation works across four interconnected areas — because strong communities require strong businesses, real belonging, economic resilience, and earned trust.

  • Supporting innovations that help local businesses thrive and stay connected to their communities
  • Funding initiatives that foster real-world belonging and give people genuine reasons to show up
  • Ensuring technological progress — including AI — strengthens local communities rather than hollowing them out
  • Building trust through privacy-first principles and real-world accountability

We believe society flourishes when prosperity is shared and reinvested locally — where families thrive, small businesses grow, and young people see a positive path forward.
