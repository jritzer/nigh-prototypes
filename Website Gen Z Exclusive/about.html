About Nigh

In real life,
presence is the point.

Nigh exists to help people create and participate in moments worth showing up for in real life.

It’s built on a simple belief: Communities are strongest when people participate in them.

Today, we spend more time connected to screens than ever before, yet many people feel less connected to each other, their communities, and the world around them.

We believe technology should help people participate more in real life — not retreat further from it.

A way for artists, organizers, and friends — along with the businesses and venues — to create and participate in spontaneous real-world experiences that only exist when people show up.

We believe the real world is ready for a comeback. Not by replacing the digital world, but by reminding us what makes real life meaningful: presence, participation, and shared experiences.

Real people. Real places. Real moments. Real soon.
