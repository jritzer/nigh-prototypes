FAQ

Common questions

What is a Nigh Drop?

▼
A Nigh Drop is an exclusive, time-specific real-world experience that motivates people to show up in person. Every Drop is tied to a real place, a real time, and a real offering — not a discount or a deal.

Who can host a Nigh Drop?

▼
Any brick-and-mortar business — restaurants, retail stores, fitness studios, salons, venues, service providers, entertainment spaces. If you have a physical location where people can show up, you can host a Drop.

Is this a deal or coupon platform?

▼
No. Nigh Drops are designed to elevate your brand and pricing power, not compress margins. They create experiences worth attending — not deals that train customers to wait for discounts.

Can Drops be scheduled in advance?

▼
Yes. Drops can be planned and scheduled ahead of time, or launched spontaneously. You have full control over when your Drop goes live.

Do people need the app?

▼
People can preview Nigh Drops in a browser, but to claim a ticket and unlock proximity-aware features, they use the Nigh Drops app — available on the App Store and Google Play.

How do people find out about Drops?

▼
Through the Nigh Drops app, push notifications, QR codes, shared links, and word of mouth. No algorithm decides who sees your Drop — it's proximity and presence-based.

What does a proof-of-attendance ticket look like?

▼
Each ticket is a unique, animated digital collectible that proves you were there. Think of it as a memory artifact — not just a receipt.
