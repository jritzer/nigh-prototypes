How Nigh Drops Work

Simple for Hosts. Exciting for Guests.

Nigh gives hosts a simple way to bring people together in real life — without relying on ads, algorithms, or endless feeds.

01

Create a Drop

A host creates an exclusive experience at a time they want guests to show up.

02

Spread the Word

They share it — via link, QR code, online, text, and directly to their followers.

03

People Secure A Spot

Guests get a ticket to reserve their spot before it's too late.

04

They Show Up IRL

Ticket holders arrive at the set time and experience the moment in person.
