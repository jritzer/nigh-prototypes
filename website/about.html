About Nigh

In real life,
presence is the point.

Nigh exists to help businesses and brands create moments people show up for in real life. It's built on a simple belief: the real world is still where connection, culture, and value matter most.

We believe the real world is ready for a comeback. Not by replacing the digital world, but by reminding us what we've been missing.

Real places. Real people. Real moments, at the right time.

Nigh means near, in place, in time, and in relationship. That's where the best things in life happen.
Built in Boulder, Colorado. Designed to keep the real world simple, grounded, and fun.
