Change is Nigh.

It's Time to Bring Back Local.

The Nigh Foundation advances innovations that strengthen local communities, real-life belonging, and shared prosperity in a rapidly changing world. We believe what matters most in life should feel close — close to home, close to opportunity, close to community.

Why We Exist

Real connection and community happens in person.

It happens across tables, in classrooms, in community centers, and at local stores, restaurants, studios, and venues. We build trust through shared space, conversation, laughter, eye contact, and collaboration. Technology can enhance that connection — but it cannot replace it.

Over time, opportunity, economic power, and decision-making have drifted away from local communities. Many families feel stretched. Many young people feel uncertain about their future. Many local businesses struggle to compete in systems optimized for scale rather than shared prosperity.

Strong local economies are the bedrock of thriving societies. When local businesses succeed, communities gain stability, dignity, and hope. When more value circulates locally, society becomes stronger from the ground up.

Rebuilding strong local economies is not a rejection of innovation. It is the next step in human advancement — aligning technology and investment with broader prosperity and collective well-being.

Our Focus Areas

What we're building toward

The Foundation works across four interconnected areas — because strong communities require strong businesses, real belonging, economic resilience, and earned trust.

🏪

Local Business Vitality

Support innovations that help local businesses become more profitable, more connected to their communities, and more resilient to economic disruption. When local businesses thrive, everyone nearby benefits.

🤝

Real World Belonging

Fund initiatives that foster real-world community and give people genuine reasons to show up in-real-life. Real-world connection and belonging are not a feature — they are a critical foundation.

🌱

Community Resilience

The same technological forces reshaping the economy can either hollow out local communities or strengthen them. The Foundation is focused on ensuring that progress — including AI — is directed toward broader prosperity, not concentrated at the top.

🛡

Safety & Trust

Design community initiatives with privacy-first principles and real-world accountability at their core. Trust is built in person, over time — and technology should protect it, not exploit it.

Our Mission

Technology that works for everyone.

Nigh Foundation works to ensure technological progress benefits everyone by prioritizing the strengthening of local economies and communities. We do this by:

  • Supporting innovations that help local businesses become more profitable and sustainable
  • Funding programs and research that demonstrate how technology can reinforce real-world human connection
  • Promoting solutions that circulate more economic value back into local communities

We believe society flourishes when prosperity is reinvested locally — where families thrive, small businesses grow, and young people see a positive path forward.
